How these two partners work together

The First Track model uses Skieasy as a preparation layer and Lovell (or future Alpine partners) as the delivery layer. This combination is what makes our trips genuinely different — participants don't waste their first two days on snow learning to stop.

Skieasy → Before the trip

Participants train in London — on a revolving ski treadmill — building muscle memory and technique before they ever step on a chair lift. They arrive in the Alps already able to stop and turn.

Lovell → During the trip

Better-prepared participants progress faster in the Alps. Instructors spend less time on basics and more time on technique refinement. Everyone improves more in the same week.

🏔️ Alpine Partner

Lovell Camp & Academy

📍 Schönried / Gstaad, Switzerland

Lovell Camp & Academy is a second-generation, family-run mountain lodge and ski school in Schönried, near Gstaad, Switzerland — owned and operated by Bryce and Victoria Lovell, formerly directors of the John F. Kennedy International School in Saanen. Their deep roots in education and alpine instruction make them a natural fit for First Track's school and youth programmes.

The Mountain Lodge is an 18-room, en-suite facility sleeping approximately 70 guests, located just three minutes from the slopes with its own minibus fleet. This is not a generic hotel — it's a purpose-built group ski accommodation with everything on-site and structured around the daily rhythm of intensive on-snow learning.

Lovell already works with established UK operators like The Ski Company, so they understand and welcome the third-party organiser model. For First Track, they represent additional demand — a complementary channel, not a competing one.

🎿 London Prep Partner

Skieasy at Dukes Meadows

📍 Chiswick, London W4

Skieasy is London's specialist indoor ski school — based at Dukes Meadows Golf & Tennis in Chiswick — offering 1:1 ski instruction on a revolving ski treadmill. Year-round, weatherproof, and proven to accelerate technique, it's the most efficient way to build real skiing foundations before a trip to the Alps.

For First Track, Skieasy plays two distinct roles: as a pre-trip preparation partner for school groups and individual bookers, and as a source of motivated adult and youth clients who have already committed to improving their skiing and are ready for the next step on real snow.

Skieasy's client base is self-selecting — people who choose to ski on a treadmill in west London are exactly the kind of motivated learners First Track programmes are designed for. Our partnership creates a natural progression pathway: train in London, ski in the Alps.

What makes Skieasy different

🔄

Revolving ski treadmill

A continuous moving slope — like a treadmill for skiing — that lets instructors control speed and gradient, focusing on exactly the technique the skier needs to develop.

🪞

Mirrored walls for self-analysis

Skiers can watch their own technique in real time — a powerful tool for correcting posture, weight distribution, and edge control that slope lessons can't replicate.

☁️

Weatherproof, year-round

No snow required. Beginners can start learning in July and arrive in the Alps in December already confident. Schools can book PE/games slots without weather dependency.

👶

Ages from 4 (ski) and 7 (snowboard)

Suitable for the youngest school groups — a realistic pre-trip preparation option even for primary school trips.
