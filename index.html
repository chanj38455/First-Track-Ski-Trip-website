Our Philosophy

More than a ski holiday. A week that changes how you ski.

Most ski holidays involve queuing for lessons between meals and repeating the same run until confidence fades. First Track is different. We build every trip around deliberate, structured progression — the same principles that underpin elite athletic development, applied to the slopes.

Whether you're a school group taking to snow for the first time, a teen looking to break through a plateau, or an adult who trained indoors and is ready for real Alpine terrain — we match you with the right instruction, the right environment, and the right partners.

Why First Track

What sets a First Track week apart

🎯

Deliberately structured

Every day is designed around progression, not aimless runs. Participants arrive with a goal and leave with measurable improvement in technique and confidence.

🤝

Handpicked partners

We work only with instruction and lodge partners who meet our standard — Lovell Camp & Academy in Gstaad and Skieasy in London are the benchmark we hold everyone to.

🔒

Compliance you can rely on

Operating on the STF/LOtC pathway with full financial protection, public liability insurance, DBS-checked staff and a written safeguarding policy. Schools can book with confidence.

🌍

Alps-wide reach

From Gstaad in Switzerland to resorts across France, Austria and Italy — we match your group to the right destination for their budget, ability, and ambitions.

📋

End-to-end management

Transfers, accommodation, instruction, lift passes, evening activities and parent communications. You focus on the experience; we handle the logistics.

🏠

Indoor preparation

Our partnership with Skieasy means beginners arrive on snow already able to stop and turn — improving safety, confidence and the group's collective progression rate.
